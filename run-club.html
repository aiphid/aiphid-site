03 / WHAT IT IS

Three things,
at once.

Not a fun run with a DJ at the end. Not a festival with a 5K bolted on. One choreographed thing — built for runners who go out.

A

A real 5K

Closed-course, chip-timed, USATF-style 5K route through the host city's flagship park. PR-able. Not a fun run.

B

A live set

Resident DJs ride the course on a slow-moving rig. Tempo shifts with the mile splits. You hear the build at the hill, the drop at the finish.

C

An after-party

Cross the line, get a wristband, walk twenty feet. Open until noon. Coffee, electrolytes, and a headline set.
